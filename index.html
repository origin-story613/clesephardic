Who We Are

The Cleveland Sephardic Community is a warm, family-oriented Sephardic minyan located in Beachwood inside the Waxman Chabad. We follow a Moroccan siddur while embracing the diversity of Sephardic and Mizrahi traditions.

Our congregation is a mosaic of the Jewish world, with families hailing from Bukhara, Morocco, Iraq, Turkey, Ethiopia, Persia, and beyond. We offer a welcoming place to pray and connect with authentic tradition.
