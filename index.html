Cleveland Sephardic Community

"Your Sephardic Home in Cleveland"

Cleveland Sephardic Community is a warm, family-oriented Sephardic minyan in Cleveland. We follow a Moroccan siddur while embracing the beautiful diversity of Sephardic and Mizrahi traditions.

Our congregation is a mosaic of the Jewish world, with families hailing from Bukhara, Morocco, Iraq, Turkey, Ethiopia, Persia, and beyond. Whether you are a local family, a student, or a visitor, we offer a welcoming place to pray and connect with authentic Sephardic tradition.
