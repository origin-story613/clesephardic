Who and What We Are

The Cleveland Sephardic Congregation is a vibrant community in Beachwood dedicated to the preservation of our shared heritage. We provide a warm and welcoming environment for prayer, learning, and growth.

Our congregation is a beautiful mosaic of the Jewish world.

Whether your family’s traditions hail from Bukhara, Morocco, Iraq, Turkey, Ethiopia, or Persia, you will find a home with us. We take pride in the diversity of our members, coming together as one heart to maintain the timeless melodies and customs of the Edot HaMizrach.
